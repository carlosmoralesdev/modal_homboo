About Homboo

Every homeowner deserves to realize their dream home with unparalleled architecture, shaped by their visions and needs in a dynamic world of constant social, technological, and environmental change. Thus, we created a portal that promotes and sells online architects' original house designs to all future homeowners, and we called it Homboo (home + Booster).

At Homboo, we sell online house designs/floor plans that blend the best architecture with the latest innovations, ensuring each home stands as a testament to individual aspirations.

FAQ

Ownership of Home Designs Ownership of Home Designs

Homboo hosts the next generation of home architecture projects from global designers, but it doesn't own or create these designs.

House Design Project Quality Assurance House Design Project Quality Assurance

House design projects are uploaded by the architects themselves. Our team reviews each project to ensure it meets Homboo's quality and completeness standards.

Content of Purchased Projects Content of Purchased Projects

Projects include a complete architectural drawing set with essential and general construction drawings details. Additional documentation may vary.

Measurement Systems Measurement Systems

Architects will provide house design projects in Imperial systems, but they may also include projects in the metric system.

Additional Documentation Additional Documentation

Beyond the delivered Architectural construction plan set, projects will generally require separate sets for structural, electrical, mechanical, and plumbing details.
