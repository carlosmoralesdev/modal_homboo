FAQ

Joining Requirements Joining Requirements

Homboo is a platform that warmly welcomes all architects, valuing their unique contributions. We do have certain standards in place to ensure the quality of our community, but rest assured, all applications are carefully reviewed.

Individual vs. Office Registration Individual vs. Office Registration

At Homboo, we believe in allowing architects to choose how they want to participate in our platform. You can join as an individual or as part of an architecture firm/organization.

Publishing a Project Publishing a Project

Register, submit the required professional information, and then upload a project via the platform's uploading service. Registration information and uploaded projects undergo team review before publication.

Design Styles Design Styles

At homboo, we strive to offer customers the next generation of home designs. We encourage architects to upload modern and contemporary house designs. Please keep in mind homeowners' needs and requirements to meet the demand.

Requirements for a house design Project Requirements for a house design Project

At Homboo, we urge architects to upload and sell projects designed in wood framing. All house designs must include the information needed to build them correctly. It comprises the house's general foundation floor plan, design type, scaled floor plans/elevations/sections, exterior and interior finish materials and construction details, bathroom fixtures/furniture, closets, and other required detailing. Engineering drawings are optional. Homboo disclaims legal responsibility in providing project design quality matters.

Copyright Ownership Copyright Ownership

Architects must own the copyright to uploaded projects. Homboo disclaims legal responsibility in copyright matters.

Earning Money Earning Money

Architects earn their set-up sell price minus Homboo's sale fee commission.

Upload Charges Upload Charges

Homboo does not charge architects for project uploads or membership!
